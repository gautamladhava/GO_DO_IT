All-round IT person

BACK TO CAREERS & VACANCIES

What you look like

You need to be able to script, preferably in Java, Python, etc.

You need to be able to understand how to integrate systems, so what kind of triggers might be needed to initiate data-exchange, what kinds of controls you might need to put in place to handle data-exchange, how you will deal with exceptions.

You will need to learn or have experience with managing Microsoft Azure tenancies.

You will have more than a passing interest in and knowledge of IOS & Android.

You will need to be someone who finds it easy to quickly understand new systems & applications.

You will definitely need to be someone who is interested in a varied set of challenges, that does not like coming in to work and doing the same repetitive tasks.

What we expect you to do

Support your colleagues who will regularly need you to perform minor miracles in helping them get systems to talk and data to flow, predictably, reliably and securely for our customers. You will also need to have more than a passing interest in IoT and how data from sensors in the field can be captured, processed and used to trigger events within systems and applications.

Of course, we will also expect you to keep an eye on our own cloud systems operation – essentially Office365, Azure and a few specific line-of-business solutions, as well as be responsible for finding, recommending and implementing the platform and tools we work with.

What we will give you

Aside from the obvious primary and secondary benefits mentioned in our careers page, your principal benefits will be;

  • A supportive environment that allows you to grow, learn and develop
  • Knowledgeable and interesting colleagues who keep you challenged
  • The ability to shape your own and help shape our future
  • Recognition as being an essential part of the success of our business

Invitation to apply

We aren’t going to list a multitude of certifications or skills that you need to have to do the job. We are looking for a mix of skills, intelligence and communication skills. We do expect you to be able to support your ability to fulfill the requirements we have detailed, but we aren’t going to put a pre-requisite on the number of years of experience of doing so.

If you think the job sounds like something that you really want to do, and the company sounds like one you’d like to work for, then you should apply.
